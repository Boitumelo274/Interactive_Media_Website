Shaped by Sound. Defined by Hip-Hop.

A digital story of the music,artist, and albums that influence who I am.

My identity is shaped by the Hip-Hop I grew up with, the artists who spoke to me, the albums that influenced the way I think, and the culture that helped me find my voice. Hip-Hop became more than music; it became a guide for how I see myself and the world. It continues to shape my confidence, creativity, and personal expression.

This website highlights the music I connect with and the rappers who inspire me, showing the sounds that have fueled my growth and shaped my personality. Each song and artist reflects a piece of my journey and the mindset I've built. Together, they tell the story of how Hip-Hop continues to influence my life.
